⚑ HOW TO PLAY

Your Term Begins

Read the scenario
Each month a real civic issue lands on your desk. The principle shown at the top tells you which Catholic Social Teaching value is at stake.

Choose A, B or C
Every choice has trade-offs. Option A is bold but costly. Option B is the refusal — it has consequences too. Option C is a compromise, but never a free ride.

Watch your six stats
Faith, Economy, People, Environment, Justice, and Infrastructure. If any one stat hits zero, your term ends early. Keep them all alive.

Some choices trigger follow-up scenarios months later. A factory approved today may send workers back to your door next year. Read carefully.
